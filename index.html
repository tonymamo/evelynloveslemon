About

Evelyn Loves Lemon is a floral design company dedicated to creating original and thoughtful designs for your personal events, home, or business.

Recognizing the power that floral arrangements can have in setting a mood, it is a critical part of the process for Evelyn Loves Lemon to get to know the client(s) and their personality. Doing so ensures a thoughtful, original design that brings your special event together in an unforgettable way. While based in Southeastern Michigan, Evelyn Loves Lemon is available for destination events upon special request.

Meet The Designer



Hello, I am Meaghan Lemon, Owner & Designer of Evelyn Loves Lemon. I have had the privilege to study Wedding and Event Design with some of the most creative designers the world has to offer. I attended the Zita Elze Design Academy located in London, England, where I studied traditional techniques while finding my own personal style.

As a designer it is important for me to bring beauty and originality to your environment. To achieve this, I make it a priority to take the time to know my client. Schedule a complimentary consultation by using the contact form below.
